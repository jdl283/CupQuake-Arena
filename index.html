Cupqake Arena

Controls

  • WASD: movement
  • left mouse button: fire
  • right mouse button: scope (with select weapons)
  • mousewheel/numbers 1-5: switch weapons

      • How to play

      Click the "Create Room" button to start a match or the "Join Room" button to enter an existing match. You can select different maps using the menu on the right side of the match selection window. In game, collect weapons and health from around the arena and fight other Cupqakes. Frag 10 other Cupqakes without dying to win. To leave a match, press escape then right click the "Leave Room" text twice.
